Features

Everything a golfer needs to outsmart the wind

Stop checking three weather apps. TeeCast synthesizes it all into one playability score you can trust.

5-Day Planning Outlook

See playability scores for every day this week. Each day is broken into four tee-time windows - Early, Morning, Midday, Afternoon - so you book the calmest slot.

Live Wind Evidence

Real-time readings from NOAA, Ambient Weather, and Visual Crossing stations around your course. See which stations agree and which are outliers.

Playability Scores

One clear 0–100 number that factors in wind speed, gusts, direction stability, and transitions. No more guessing if 12 mph with 18 mph gusts is "playable."

Favorites at a Glance

Save your home courses and see today's scores, weather, wind direction, and best day this week - all from the home screen. No tapping required.

Station-Level Transparency

No black-box forecasts. See every station's reading, its distance from the course, data freshness, and whether it's classified as supporting, outlier, or stale.

Microclimate Aware

Terrain-separated stations (mountains, coastal barriers) are automatically excluded. You get wind data that actually represents your course, not the airport 3 miles away.

Privacy Policy

Effective March 17, 2026

TeeCast is a golf weather application that helps golfers plan tee times using wind and weather data. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Location Data

When you search for a golf course or view weather conditions, the app uses the course's geographic coordinates to fetch weather data from third-party services. We do not access your device's GPS or track your physical location.

Locally Stored Data

TeeCast stores the following data on your device using browser local storage:

  • Favorite courses you save for quick access
  • Settings preferences (units, wind sensitivity)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Account Information (Future)

We plan to introduce optional user accounts for premium features. When available, account creation may require an email address and password. This policy will be updated before that feature launches.

Analytics

TeeCast uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screen views, feature usage, and general device information. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out of analytics collection through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

TeeCast retrieves weather and course data from:

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Device GPS or real-time physical location
  • Names, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Advertising identifiers

Data Storage & Security

All preferences and favorites are stored locally on your device. TeeCast does not operate a backend server that stores user data. Weather data is cached temporarily in browser memory to reduce API calls.

Children's Privacy

TeeCast does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as new features are added. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since TeeCast stores data only on your device, you can clear it at any time through your browser's local storage settings or the app's settings.

California residents: TeeCast does not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.
